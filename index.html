2. Which of the following is NOT a physical sign of intoxication?

2. 以下哪項不是醉酒的生理徵狀？

a) Red/glassy eyes 紅/呆滯嘅眼睛

b) Swaying or stumbling 搖晃或踉蹌

c) Speaking quietly 輕聲說話

d) Excessive sweating 過度出汗

Correct Answer: c) Speaking quietly

正確答案：c) 輕聲說話

Explanation: Speaking quietly is not a physical sign of intoxication. In fact, intoxicated individuals often speak louder than necessary.

解釋：輕聲說話唔係醉酒嘅身體表現。事實上，醉酒嘅人通常會講嘢大聲過頭。

b) False 錯誤
